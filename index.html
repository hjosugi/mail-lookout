Mail Lookout

A send-confirmation add-in for Outlook. It helps review recipients, attachments, and message content before mail leaves your draft.

Download manifest Try emulator GitHub

Recipients

Review To, Cc, and Bcc recipients before Outlook sends the message.

Attachments

Confirm real attachments and catch messages that mention missing files.

Body

Pause for a short review of the message body and empty-subject warning.
